SCHEDULE FOR THIS YATRA !

Day 1 - Morning 10AM Depature from Dehradun to Yamunotri. we'll stays here to view mountains.
Day 2 - Then we'll go to Gangotri after Darshan.We'll stay in Gangotri. Day 3 - In morning 9AM we'll Depature to kedarnath dham then we'll stays here.
Day 4 - In morning we'll Complete our Darshan. And get some time for photoshoot,Shopping,etc. And we'll live here for 1 day.
Day 5 - In morning we'll Depature to Sonprayag and stays in Sonprayag.
Day 6 - In morning at 10AM we'll Depatureto Devprayag and here we stays in Devprayag to see here's view.
Day 7 - Here we'll come to Rushikesh and we'll stays here to see ganga aarti and parmarth niketan.
Day 8 - After that we depature to our starting depature place means Dehradun.
